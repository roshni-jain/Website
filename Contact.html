Frequently Asked Questions.

If you have,we have answers for you. Please refer to the table below for any questions you might have. They might help you better if you are visiting our website for the first time. Don't forget to contact us for further queries. We love to Bundle Up!

Questions Answers
1. Can I register online ? Yes, you can it is very simple. just visit the get started page and follow the instructions.
2. Is there a fee associated with signup for an account ? No, it is a free to set up an account. You only have to pay if you want to upgrade for business or an enterprise.
3. What if I can't sign up for an account due to technical issues. What shoould I do ? You can email us and one of our associates will be able to help you right away.
4. Can I split the cost of my bundle with the family ? Yes,you can add people from your family onto the plan and the cost will be divided eqaully.
5. What is the maximum number of subscriptions I can have in my Bundle ? The maximum number of subscriptions in one account are 30. After that you would either create a new account or upgrade to business account.
6. Can I cancel only some of my subscriptions? Yes, you can do this by either contacting us or by logging into your account and clicking on the manage subscriptions button.
7. Do I have to pay for my subscriptions on the first of every month ? No, you can set up a different date for your payment. You can also enroll in payment plans if you want.
8. Can I access my account anywhere in the world? No, Unfortunately you can only access your account in the United States.
